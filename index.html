What Our Clients Are Saying About Us

ET-Touil Abdelilah

Team Member

The inspiration for my Property Management Project came from observing the daily struggles faced by property owners and tenants. As someone who has seen the challenges of managing multiple properties and dealing with tenants' needs, I felt the need for a solution that would streamline this process. I wanted to build a platform that empowers property owners, maintainers, and tenants by centralizing tasks like rent payments, maintenance requests, and lease tracking into one simple interface.
This project is also part of my portfolio for Holberton School, where I aim to demonstrate my ability to solve practical, real-world problems using technology. You can learn more about Holberton School
